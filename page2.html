These are tools actually used by remote workers, freelancers, and content creators. All have a generous free tier to get you started.

✍️ Writing & Content Tools

Google Docs FREE

Word processor with real-time collaboration, cloud saving, and comment features. Perfect for freelance writers, VAs, and remote teams. No Microsoft Office needed.

Grammarly FREE / PRO

Checks grammar, spelling, tone, and clarity in real-time. The free version is powerful enough for most writers. Essential for non-native English writers.

Hemingway Editor FREE

Highlights complex sentences and passive voice. Makes your writing cleaner and easier to read. Great for blog posts and landing pages.

ChatGPT / Claude AI FREE

AI assistants for drafting, brainstorming, research summaries, code generation, and more. Don't publish AI content without editing — use it as a starting point.

🎨 Design & Creative Tools

Canva FREE / PRO

Drag-and-drop design tool for social media graphics, presentations, thumbnails, logos, and more. Thousands of free templates. The free plan is very capable for freelancers.

GIMP FREE

Professional-grade image editing, completely free. Steeper learning curve than Canva but far more powerful for advanced photo editing and manipulation.

DaVinci Resolve FREE

Hollywood-level video editing software at zero cost. Used by professional video editors. Free version has almost everything most creators need.

Figma FREE / PRO

Industry-standard UI/UX design tool. Free for individuals with up to 3 projects. If you want to get into UI/UX design freelancing, start here.

📊 Productivity & Organisation

Notion FREE

All-in-one workspace for notes, project management, databases, and wikis. Great for managing client projects, content calendars, and business planning. Many freelancers also sell Notion templates.

Trello FREE

Visual project management using boards, lists, and cards. Easier to learn than Notion. Perfect for tracking freelance projects and deadlines.

Google Workspace (Sheets, Drive, Calendar) FREE

Google Sheets replaces Excel for most needs. Drive provides 15GB free cloud storage. Calendar for scheduling client calls. A complete free office suite.

Clockify FREE

Time-tracking tool for freelancers. Track hours per project, generate reports, and create accurate invoices. Completely free with unlimited tracking.

💳 Getting Paid Online

PayPal FREE to receive

Most widely used for international freelance payments. Easy to set up. Fees apply when withdrawing in certain currencies — check your country's rates.

Wise (formerly TransferWise) FREE account

Best option for receiving international payments with low conversion fees. Can hold multiple currencies. Particularly good if you earn in USD or EUR but live elsewhere.

Payoneer FREE account

Widely used by freelancers on platforms like Upwork and Fiverr. Provides a US bank account number, which makes receiving payments from international clients easier.

📣 Marketing & Growth Tools

Mailchimp FREE up to 500 contacts

Email marketing platform. Build an email list to stay in touch with clients and customers. Email lists are one of the most valuable assets for any online business.

Buffer / Later FREE plans

Schedule social media posts in advance. Useful if social media management is part of your service or you want to grow your own audience without being online all day.

Google Analytics FREE

Track website visitors, traffic sources, popular pages, and user behaviour. Essential for any blog or website. Clients often want to see this data — knowing how to read it is a valuable skill.

Ubersuggest / Google Keyword Planner FREE

Find keywords people search for online. Useful for SEO writing, blog planning, and understanding what content will attract visitors. Basic version of both is free.
